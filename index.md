Minna no nihongo
================= 

Minna no nihongo (みんなの日本語) is a popular series of Japanese language textbooks used worldwide for teaching Japanese as a foreign language. The series is designed for beginners and covers various aspects of the Japanese language, including grammar, vocabulary, reading, writing, and conversation skills.
